About Me

Crafting an "About Me" section has been surprisingly challenging, as I've always found it difficult to talk about myself. As an introverted overthinker, finding the "right words to say" has never came easy.

So, let's start with a simple question: Why did I delve into the world of tech? Unlike the typical narrative of tinkering with computers at a young age, my childhood was filled with adventures outdoors, creative pursuits like drawing, and the simple joy of childhood games. Tech wasn't anywhere near the radar.

My first brush with tech came during some college credit courses around 2014, where I learned HTML and gained a basic understanding of networking. But it wasn't until later, about a year ago, after embarking on a career as a massage therapist, that my journey in tech truly commenced. While I cherished the ability to alleviate others' pain through massage, it eventually took a toll on my own well-being.

Fortuitously, an opportunity arose to transition into a role as a receptionist at the spa where I worked. Tasked with managing social media, I discovered a passion for design through platforms like Canva, which led me to delve deeper into tools like Illustrator and Photoshop.

Through online learning platforms like Skillshare, I stumbled upon the world of web design and development at an online bootcamp called Skillcrush. What started as an exploration soon transformed into a full-blown passion. The moment I began coding, I felt a sense of fulfillment and excitement that I hadn't experienced before. Switching gears to focus on front-end web development, I found my calling.

Now entrenched in the world of tech, my aim is to bring the same sense of relief and satisfaction to others that I once provided as a massage therapist. I'm driven by the desire to identify problems and deliver meaningful solutions.
