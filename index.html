About Me

Surprisingly enough, crafting this "About Me" section was the most challenging part of this portfolio, as I've always found it difficult to talk about myself. As an introverted overthinker, finding the "right words to say" has never come easy.

So, I'll start with a simple question: Why tech? Unlike the narrative of tinkering with computers at a young age, my childhood was filled with tree climbing, bug catching, and endless amounts of drawing. Tech wasn't anywhere near the radar but being creative and open-minded was and forever will be.

My first brush with tech came around 2014, during some college credit courses, where I learned HTML and gained a basic understanding of how computers work. While those classes were intriguing, I chose to explore other career paths. For 9 years, I searched aimlessly for something that I could never quite figure out. It was like walking into a room and immediately forgetting what you went in there to do just to circle back and still not know what it was. That's what life felt like up until a little over year ago when I finally figured out what I was searching for.

Passion. I was searching for my passion, my purpose, and I found that in tech, specifically front-end web development.

At the end of 2022, after embarking on a new career as a massage therapist, my journey in tech truly commenced. While I cherished the ability to alleviate others' pain through massage and education, it eventually took a toll on my physical well-being.

Fortunately, an opportunity arose to transition into a role as a receptionist at the spa where I worked. Tasked with managing social media, I discovered that passion. Through platforms like Canva, which led me to delve deeper into tools like Illustrator and Photoshop, I discovered how to express my creativity through tech.

Down the rabbit hole of online learning platforms like Skillshare, I stumbled upon the world of web design and development at an online bootcamp called Skillcrush. What started as an exploration soon became full circle, as I found myself once again learning HTML. The moment I began coding again, I felt a sense of fulfillment and excitement that I hadn't experienced before. Switching gears to focus on front-end web development, I knew exactly what I was looking for all this time. Tech offers an endless array of possibilities and outlets for my creative side.

My aim, my purpose, in tech is to find solutions that resolve stress for others. My goal is to collaborate and inspire others with the projects that I'm a part of. I'm driven by the hope that one day I'll inspire others who've been aimlessly searching for that missing part of their life and find their passion like I did.

Take a look through my projects and if something catches your eye, reach out. I'd love to know your thoughts and discuss how we can benefit from each other.
